What is it?

Video Miam is a small website that shows on a single page all the videos you might be interested in watching, based on the channels you follow. It was born from a frustration with Youtube's interface that makes it more difficult than it should be to see videos you haven't seen yet from your favorite channels.

Why try it?

  • No shady recommendation algorithm: only videos from your channels, sorted by release date.
  • Fine-grained control: subscribe to playlists instead of whole channels.
  • Free your memory: you no longer need to remember which videos you've already watched, and which you haven't.
  • Open source: if you want to, you can even host your own instance of Video Miam Link to github.
